Cook's Assistant

Difficulty: Novice Requirements: Beginner Length: Short

Start location: Speak to the chef in the Lumbridge Castle Kitchen. The chef does not have all the ingredients for his cake and needs your help!

Requirements:

Items: A Empty pot (can be purchased from the Lumbridge General Store for a few coins)

NPCs: Cook -Lumbridge-, Gillie Groats, Millie Miller

Walkthrough hide »

Starting out

After speaking to the chef, you will need to collect three ingredients for his cake: a bucket of top-quality milk, a super large egg and a pot of some extra fine flour. Refer to the map below to obtain these ingredients.

Getting the Milk

Head to the cow pen north-east of the Lumbridge Castle. Talk to Gillie Groats and ask for some top-quality milk. She tells you to use the special cow on the east side of the field. Pick up the bucket next to it milk the prized diary cow. You will receive a bucket of top-quality milk.

Getting the Egg

Head to Fred's farm north-west of Lumbridge. Inside the farm you will see a super large egg. Just pick it up.

Getting the Flour

Follow the path north, then west, from the Lumbridge Castle to the windmill. If you don't already have a pot, you could take the one that spawns in the windmill, or buy one from the general store on the way there. Pick one bundle of wheat from the field outside then enter the windmill. Go inside the windmill and talk to Millie Miller and ask how you can make extra fine flour. Climb to the top floor. Use your wheat with hopper, then operate the hopper controls. This will grind the wheat into wheat. Descend to the ground floor and use your pot with the flour bin. You will scoop up one pot of extra fine flour.

Back to the Chef

Bring all three items back to the chef in the Lumbridge Castle Kitchen to complete the quest.

Reward

  • 300 cooking xp
  • 500 gp
  • 20 Sardines
  • Ability to use the Lumbridge Kitchen range, which burns less food than all the other ranges in game
  • 2 Treasure Hunter keys

Quest points: 1

This quest is a requirement for:

  • Recipe For Disaster
  • Recipe For Disaster (Subquest 0: Starting Out)
  • Recipe For Disaster (Subquest 1 : Dwarf)
  • Recipe For Disaster (Subquest 2 : Pirate Pete)
  • Recipe For Disaster (Subquest 3 : Goblins)
  • Recipe For Disaster (Subquest 4 : Skrach)
  • Recipe For Disaster (Subquest 5 : Lumbridge Guide)
  • Recipe For Disaster (Subquest 6 : Evil Dave)
  • Recipe For Disaster (Subquest 7 : Sir Amik Varze)
  • Recipe For Disaster (Subquest 8 : Awowogei)
  • Recipe For Disaster (Subquest 9 : Final Fight)

Tips, Tricks & Notes

  • The bucket respawns next to the cow and there is a pot respawn inside the windmill.
  • If you have already completed the quest without receiving the updated rewards, you can talk to Chef and get 500 gp and 20 sardines
